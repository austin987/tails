If you want to talk publicly to T(A)ILS developpers or users:
  • you may want to join our [[chatroom|chat]];
  • the [[forum]] is open for discussions that do not belong to bugs, feature requests and the like;
  • you can [subscribe](https://boum.org/mailman/listinfo/tails-dev/) or write to the tails-dev@boum.org mailing-list; any message sent to this list is stored in a public archive, so beware of what your email content and headers reveal about yourself: location, IP address, etc.

For matters that need to be hidden from the public eyes, you can send email to the private development mailing list: amnesia@boum.org; encrypting such email with our [[GnuPG key]] is the only way to achieve end-to-end encryption.
