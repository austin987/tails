[[!meta title="Privacy for anyone anywhere"]]

Tails is a [[!wikipedia desc="live operating system" Live_USB]], that you can start on almost any computer from a DVD, USB stick, or SD card. It aims at preserving your privacy and anonymity, and helps you to:

testing
  • use the Internet anonymously and circumvent censorship;
    all connections to the Internet are forced to go through [[the Tor network|https://www.torproject.org/]];
  • leave no trace on the computer you are using unless you ask it explicitly;
  • use state-of-the-art cryptographic tools to encrypt your files, emails and instant messaging.
